Venezuela Maduro Plans Shift to Fully Digitalized Economy

Venezuela is planning to move to a fully digital economy as hyperinflation has made worthless bolivar notes practically disappear, President Nicolas Maduro says. The US dollar has operated as an escape valve for Venezuela amid US sanctions and collapsing oil revenues.

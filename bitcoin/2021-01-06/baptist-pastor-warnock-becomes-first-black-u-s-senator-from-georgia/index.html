Baptist pastor Warnock becomes first Black U . S . Senator from Georgia

Raphael Warnock - the son of a sharecropper, who never held public office before, was the first black American senator to become Senator from the US state of Georgia. His win puts the party’s Senate candidate in the runoff election.

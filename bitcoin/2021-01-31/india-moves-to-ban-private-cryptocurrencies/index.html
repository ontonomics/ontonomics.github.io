India moves to ban private cryptocurrencies

The Indian government has announced plans to introduce a law to ban private cryptocurrencies such as bitcoin and put in place the framework for an official digital currency to be issued by the central bank. The BBC s Geeta Pandey spoke to the government on Friday.

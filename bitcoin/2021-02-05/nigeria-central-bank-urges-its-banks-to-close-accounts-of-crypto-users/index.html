Nigeria Central Bank urges its banks to close accounts of crypto users

Nigeria's central bank has issued a warning to financial institutions that dealing in crypto currency exchanges is "prohibited" and will face severe regulatory sanctions, according to the banking secretary Mohammadu Buhari, who is responsible for the issue.

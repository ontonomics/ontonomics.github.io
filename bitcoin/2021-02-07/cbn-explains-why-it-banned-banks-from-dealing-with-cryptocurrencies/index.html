CBN explains why it banned banks from dealing with Cryptocurrencies

Nigeria's central bank has issued a circular warning that cryptocurrencies are not being regulated until they are allowed to operate in and with entities dealing with them, including banks and other financial institutions, in the wake of the coronavirus pandemic.

Nigeria Central Bank Says Cryptocurrencies Were a Threat

Nigeria's central bank has ordered deposit-taking banks and other financial institutions to close accounts dealing in cryptocurrencies because it is threatening the country’s banking system, reports the Bloomberg investigation. Financial markets in Nigeria have been hit by a sharp fall in revenue.

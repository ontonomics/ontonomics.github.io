Crypto Traders Could Be Forced to Prove Income Under Thai Rules

Thai authorities have warned young people about their risks from cryptocurrencies, as a surge in inflows sparked concern from the countrys financial regulators, according to reports from Bloomberg. Financial advisers will be required to show income before opening trading accounts with licensed exchanges.

Dollar gains on reflation trade , Aussie recovers

The US dollar has jumped to a three-week high as investors bet on growth and inflation in the United States, while Australian Treasury yields remain below the one-year high of 1.5%. While the US Federal Reserve has warned against buying more bonds than expected, the dollar is holding below expectations.

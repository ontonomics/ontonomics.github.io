Not shutting all options on cryptocurrency , says Finance Minister Nirmala Sitharaman at India Today Conclave

India's finance minister has said that a Cabinet note is being prepared to give full details on the cryptocurrency and bitcoins in the wake of Supreme Court ruling on cryptocurrencies and other crypto-currency exchanges. However, the government has insisted that it is not shutting off all options.

Singapore warns public against crypto as world warms to Bitcoin

Singapore's Monetary Authority (MAS) has warned that cryptocurrencies are highly risky as investment products, and certainly not suitable for retail investors, according to a senior parliamentary official in the city. They have been given powers to impose additional measures on digital token service providers.

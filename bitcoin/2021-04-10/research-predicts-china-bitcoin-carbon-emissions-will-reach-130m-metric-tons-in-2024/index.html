Research Predicts China Bitcoin Carbon Emissions Will Reach 130M Metric Tons In 2024

The Bitcoin mining system is expected to increase its annual energy consumption in 2024, according to researchers in the Chinese Academy of Sciences and Tsinghua University. Scientists believe it will generate 130 million tons of carbon dioxide emissions.

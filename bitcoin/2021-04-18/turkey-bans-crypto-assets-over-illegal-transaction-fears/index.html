Turkey bans crypto assets over illegal transaction fears

Turkey’s Central Bank has banned the use of crypto assets in payments, which could cause irreparable damages for the parties to transactions, it has been announced by the country's central bank on Friday. Turkish officials say they are concerned about the impact of the new regulation.

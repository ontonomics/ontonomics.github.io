Evening Update : Ottawa in talks with Ontario over paid time off for sick employees

Canadian Prime Minister Justin Trudeau has said it is in talks with Ontario to provide paid time off for sick workers during the coronavirus pandemic.The Liberal government is planning to offer paid leave via businesses regulated by the province. The Daily Telegraph reports that the prime minister has backed up the government.

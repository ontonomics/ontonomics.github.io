ACDX Offers World First Leveraged Trading For Chia ( XCH )

The Crypto Derivatives Exchange (ACDX) is offering its users the world's first leveraged trading option for Chia Tokens (XCH) - the native tokens of Chia Network, the BitTorrent network founded by the inventor Bram Cohen. It is the first crypto exchange to kick off such a listing.

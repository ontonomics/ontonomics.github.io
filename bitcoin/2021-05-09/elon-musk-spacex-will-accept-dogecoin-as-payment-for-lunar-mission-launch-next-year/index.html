Elon Musk SpaceX will accept dogecoin as payment for lunar mission launch next year

Elon Musk’s commercial rocket company SpaceX is to launch a cryptocurrency-funded mission to the Moon in the first quarter of next year, with the company accepting the dogecoin as payment.SpaceX says it will launch the “Doge-1 Mission to The Moon”.

South Korea banking association alarmed by altcoin trading mania

Bankers in South Korea are being asked to conduct an audit on crypto exchanges, amid concerns about the risks posed by the rising number of altcoins taking on their crypto exchange clients. They are also concerned about a rise in crypto trading volumes.

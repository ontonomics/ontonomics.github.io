Russian lawmakers to allow the use of crypto in contractual payments

Russias parliament has reached an agreement with the government to allow the use of cryptocurrencies in the process of legal tenders, according to reports from the Russian Financial Times and the Invezz website. They say they are willing to make amendments to the Civil Code.

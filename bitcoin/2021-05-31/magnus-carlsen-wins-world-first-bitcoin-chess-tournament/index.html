Magnus Carlsen wins world first bitcoin chess tournament

The world's first bitcoin chess champion has won a record-breaking $60,000 (£660,000) prize for the first time in more than 20 years, according to the tournament organisers and players in the US and Canada. However, he has already lost five times when he needed it.

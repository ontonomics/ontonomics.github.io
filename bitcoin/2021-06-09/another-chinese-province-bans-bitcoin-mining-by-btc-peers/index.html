Another Chinese province bans Bitcoin mining By BTC Peers

The Chinese state of Qinghai has become the latest region to clamp down on Bitcoin mining, according to reports from the Chinese crypto news site Bitcoin PEERS. They have been ordered to suspend all existing Bitcoin operations in the Xinjiang province of China.

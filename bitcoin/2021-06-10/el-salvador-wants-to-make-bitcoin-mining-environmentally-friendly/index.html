El Salvador Wants to Make Bitcoin Mining Environmentally Friendly

El Salvador has become the first country in the world to adopt Bitcoin as a legal tender. Mining the cryptocurrency could be used in renewable energy, according to the country's officials, as part of efforts to tackle the environmental impact of Bitcoin.

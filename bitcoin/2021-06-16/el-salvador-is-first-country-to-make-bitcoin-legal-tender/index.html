El Salvador is first country to make bitcoin legal tender

El Salvador has become the first country in the world to adopt bitcoin as legal tender after Congress approved its proposal to use the cryptocurrency as a financial tender. The government says it will guarantee convertability to dollars at the time of transactions, while the US dollar will continue to be used.

Spanish Crypto Firms will Have to Run their Campaigns Past Regulator

Spanish financial regulators have ordered crypto firms to submit details of their advertising campaigns within 15 days before they are expected to launch, according to the Italian Financial Times newspaper. The government says it is planning a new crackdown on the spread of cryptocurrencies.

Britain Bans Cryptocurrency Exchange Binance From Operating in the united kingdom

Binance, the world s largest cryptocurrency exchange, has been banned from operating in the UK without its written approval, according to the Financial Conduct Authority (FCA) in Britain earlier this week. However, it is not permitted to undertake any regulated activities.

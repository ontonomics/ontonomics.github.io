UK financial watchdog bans crypto exchange Binance

Binance has been banned from trading cryptocurrencies in the UK, amid concerns about the volatility of the crypto markets, the Financial Conduct Authority (FCA) has warned. However, it is not permitted to undertake any regulated activity in Britain.

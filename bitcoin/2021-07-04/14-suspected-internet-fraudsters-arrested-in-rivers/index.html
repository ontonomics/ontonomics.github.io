14 suspected Internet fraudsters arrested in Rivers

The authorities in Rivers state have arrested 14 suspected internet fraudsters linked to fraudulent documents and bitcoin scams, the EFCC has said, following a protest in Osogbo, south-east Nigeria, on Saturday. They were also involved in the illegal transactions.

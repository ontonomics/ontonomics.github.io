Kpay Launches Mongolia First Cryptocurrency For Seamless Cross - border Payments

Mongolia's first cryptocurrency, Khaan Coin, is being rolled out to the crypto community in a bid to liberate millions of people who are left with the option of using traditional banks and financial institutions to make international payments. The team at Kpay is pleased to announce the introduction of its native currency.

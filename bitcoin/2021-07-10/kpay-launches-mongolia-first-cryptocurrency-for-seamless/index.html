Kpay Launches Mongolia First Cryptocurrency For Seamless

Mongolia's first cryptocurrency, Khaan Coin, is being launched to the crypto community in a bid to liberate millions of people from using traditional banks and financial institutions to make international payments. The team at Kpay is pleased to announce the introduction of this crypto currency.

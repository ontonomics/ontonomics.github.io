Treasury publishes draft rules for online funding

Kenya s Treasury has published draft rules to curb the loss of investor cash through unregulated investment products, including online crowdfunding schemes. The government says it will introduce strict conditions for collecting small amounts of capital from individuals to finance a business venture.

Fed Chairman Suggests That Stablecoins And Cryptocurrencies Could Become Obsolete If U . S . Digital Currency Existed

The chairman of the US Federal Reserve has warned that when the central bank launches a sovereign digital currency, it would not be forced into launching one or succumbing to outside pressure from other countries that are jumping out ahead of its launch.

UK FCA Plans $15M Campaign Warning Young Brits Over Crypto Risks

The UK’s financial watchdog has launched a digital marketing campaign to warn young people of the risks of buying cryptocurrencies and other high-risk investments. The agency has warned that younger people are likely to be drawn in by anonymous and unaccountable social media influencers.

SkyChain Closes First Tranche of June Private Placement

One of the world's biggest cryptocurrency companies has completed the first tranche of a non-brokered private placement, which it says will help fund the construction of its Birtle project located in Manitoba. The company is planning to use the net proceeds of this offering to help finance construction.

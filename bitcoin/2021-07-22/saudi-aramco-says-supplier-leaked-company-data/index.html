Saudi Aramco Says Supplier Leaked Company Data

Saudi Aramco has confirmed that its corporate data was leaked after attackers breached one of its suppliers. The company says it is investigating the leak of a limited amount of company data which was held by third-party contractors, according to Information Security Media Group.

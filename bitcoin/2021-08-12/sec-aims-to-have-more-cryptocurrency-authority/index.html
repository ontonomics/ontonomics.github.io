SEC Aims to Have More Cryptocurrency Authority

The chairman of the US Securities and Exchange Commission (SEC) has called for more authority to protect crypto trading and lending, saying it is not waiting around for Congress to act against the issue, but says he will continue to take the authorities as far as they go.

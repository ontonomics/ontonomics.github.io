New Brazilian Bitcoin ETF pledges carbon neutrality

A crypto-focused investment firm has launched a new cryptocurrency fund aimed at cutting carbon emissions from its liquid assets and reducing the amount of electricity being used in the digital currency, according to reports from the São Paulo-based B3 exchange.

Bitcoin craze reaches Honduras with first cryptocurrency ATM

Honduras has become the first country in the world to open a virtual cash machine which allows users to buy crypto-currencies using their local currency - Ethereum and Bitcoin. They are now trading at $38,700 (roughly Rs. 460,000).

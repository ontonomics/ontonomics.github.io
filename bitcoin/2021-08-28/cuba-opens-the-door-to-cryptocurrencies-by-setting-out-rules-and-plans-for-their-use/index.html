Cuba opens the door to cryptocurrencies by setting out rules and plans for their use

Cuba s government will recognise and regulate the use of cryptocurrencies in commercial transactions, according to a resolution published in its Official Gazette on Thursday.The government has announced that the rules will be enforced by the central bank. The Spanish document has been released in Cuba.

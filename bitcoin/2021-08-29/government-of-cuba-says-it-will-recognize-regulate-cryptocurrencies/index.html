Government of Cuba Says It Will Recognize , Regulate Cryptocurrencies

Cuba’s President Fidel Diez-Canel has announced that the country will regulate cryptocurrencies for payments, amid a growing crisis over the coronavirus pandemic. The announcement has been made in the wake of anti-government protests against the government.

Korean Government Says 28 Crypto Exchanges Have Met Regulatory Requirements to Continue Operations – Regulation Bitcoin News

South Korea’s financial authorities have released a list of cryptocurrency exchanges that have partially met the regulatory requirements to stay open until September 24 following the introduction of new crypto regulations in the country. Financial officials say they are unlikely to increase the number of virtual asset trading platforms.

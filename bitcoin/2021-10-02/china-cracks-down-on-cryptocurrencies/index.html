China Cracks Down On Cryptocurrencies

The Chinese government has announced a crackdown on cryptocurrency trading and banning the mining of traditional currencies, including bitcoin and other virtual currency exchanges. Financial institutions have said they will work closely to maintain the clampdown, as part of an effort to curb financial risk.

Nuvei Announces Initial Public Offering in the United States

Nuvei has announced the launch of a marketed offering of 1,500,000 subordinate voting shares in the United States and Canada, representing its initial public selling in Canada. The company is expected to apply to list it on the Toronto Stock Exchange in U.S. dollars.

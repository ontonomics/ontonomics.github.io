Kyrgyzstan Raises Electricity Rates for Cryptocurrency Miners – Mining Bitcoin News

Crypto mining businesses in Kyrgyzstan will have to pay a higher price for electricity they need to mint digital currency coins, it has been announced by the country’s authorities. They will be able to buy power using crypto currencies.

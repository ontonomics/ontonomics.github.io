Crypto exchange Binance ends derivatives trading and other offerings in South Africa

Binance, the worlds largest cryptocurrency exchange, has announced that users of its platform in South Africa will no longer be permitted to trade derivatives in compliance with local regulations. These changes could mean a loss for Africa s burgeoning digital-asset market.

El Salvador Buys the Dip and Acquires Another 420 Bitcoins

El Salvador has bought another 420 Bitcoins to take advantage of its recent price drop, according to the country's president. The price of the cryptocurrency has fallen to a record high of $66,930 in the last three months. Officials say the price is rising.

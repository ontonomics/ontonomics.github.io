Protest erupts in Dutch city on 1st night of new lockdown

Dutch media reports that hundreds of young people gathered in the northern city of Leeuwarden on Saturday night as a new coronavirus lockdown forced bars and restaurants to close at 8 p.m.In other parts of the country, riot police moved in to push them off.

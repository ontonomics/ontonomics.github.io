PM Modi Twitter handle gets hacked , tweet of legalising Bitcoin now deleted

Indian Prime Minister Narendra Modi's Twitter account has been compromised after a tweet promoting cryptocurrency was shared on the account. Officials say it is being investigated by the Ministry of Electronics and Information Technology (CERT-IN). The account is now secured.

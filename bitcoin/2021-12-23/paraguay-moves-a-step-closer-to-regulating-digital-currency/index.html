Paraguay moves a step closer to regulating digital currency

Paraguay's Deputy Chamber of Commerce has approved a new law aimed at encouraging crypto mining to operate in the country using surplus electricity generated by the creation of virtual assets and cryptocurrency tokens. They will be sent to the House of Commons for further deliberation.

Spain Reduces ATM Numbers to 2002 Levels as Country Moves to Digital Payments – News Bitcoin News

The number of ATMs in Spain has dropped to its lowest level in more than a decade, according to the Bank of Spain, the latest figures show. They have been removed from the network for the first time in nearly 20 years, but they are still being used.

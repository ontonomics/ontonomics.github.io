Grammy Awards pushed to April 3 and moved from LA to Las Vegas

The 2021 Grammy Awards have been postponed due to the coronavirus pandemic, organisers have confirmed. The ceremony will be broadcast from the MGM Grand Garden Arena in Las Vegas, in a bid to raise awareness of the impact of Covid-19 on the event.

Bill Introduced To Make Bitcoin A Legal Tender In Arizona

The US Senate has proposed to make bitcoin a legal tender in the US state of Arizona.The bill says the digital currency could be lawful medium of exchange there, enabling citizens to pay debts, public charges, taxes and dues with bitcoin.

New York Delays a Decision on Permits for Bitcoin Miner Power Plant

A decision on whether to allow Bitcoin mining operations to continue at a power plant in New York has been delayed until March 31, the US Department of Environmental Conservation (DEC) has confirmed. Climate activists have been closely watching the decision, the BBC understands.

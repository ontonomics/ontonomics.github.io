El Salvador plans to issue first bitcoin bond next month

El Salvador has announced plans to introduce a compulsory oversubscription of shares in its first Bitcoin bonds, which will be issued on the cryptocurrency-based liquidation service Liquid, in the US and Canada, next month. They will also be published online.

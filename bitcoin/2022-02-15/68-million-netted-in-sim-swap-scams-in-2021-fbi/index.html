$68 Million Netted In SIM Swap Scams In 2021 : FBI

US authorities have reported a record number of complaints about phone SIM swap scams between 2018 and 2020, the FBI has confirmed. They claimed the total of $68 million lost in the three-year period alone, according to US media.

DOJ Touts New Cryptocurrency Crackdown Team

The US Department of Justice (DOJ) has announced a new FBI unit to tackle the abuse of virtual currency, cryptocurrency and other crypto-currency technologies, as part of an international crackdown on cyber-crime. They are also named as the first director of the NCET.

Tesla , Block and Blockstream to mine bitcoin off solar power in Texas

Tesla, Block and Block have claimed they will mine bitcoin off solar power in the US, according to a new US record - the first such venture in more than two decades. These are the latest examples of how ecological concerns affected bitcoin mining.

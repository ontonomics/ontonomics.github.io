ETF Securities launches bitcoin and ethereum ETFs

Two Australian firms have announced they will launch their first direct exposure to crypto-based investments (ETFs) in the country, in a bid to boost the growth of the crypto currency market, bitcoin and ethereum. They will also list the first cryptocurrency in Australia.

Central African Republic Becomes Second Country to Adopt Bitcoin

The Central African Republic has become the second country in the world to adopt bitcoin as official money, a move which is expected to open up new opportunities for the country’s economy, the government has said, as it tries to raise awareness of cryptocurrencies.

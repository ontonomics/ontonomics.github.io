Central African Republic authorises cryptocurrency | Borneo Bulletin Online

The Central African Republic has become the first country in Africa to recognise the use of Bitcoin as a reference currency, according to the country’s parliamentary committee on commerce and banking, the BBC understands. They say they are concerned about the impact of the cryptocurrency.

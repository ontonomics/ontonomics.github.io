Coinbase Makes Changes to Prevent Front Running on Token Listings

Coinbase says it is considering listing new cryptocurrency tokens in a bid to stop insider trading, amid concerns about the possibility of being listed on the exchange. The company has said it would not be able to guess, by examining its software.

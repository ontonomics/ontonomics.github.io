Gucci To Start Accepting Crypto Payments In Select US Stores

Luxury fashion house Gucci has announced it will accept payments in cryptocurrencies in its North American stores this summer, according to reports from the Gulf Business agency. They will also accept Bitcoin, Dogecoin and Shiba Inu in the US and Canada.

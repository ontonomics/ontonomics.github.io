China now world 2nd - biggest bitcoin miner after activity bounced back from government crackdown

China has made a comeback as the global bitcoin mining powerhouse, despite Beijing's crypto crackdown, according to research published by the Financial Conduct Authority (CCAF) in the journal The New York Times. Mining experts say the country is now the second-largest location for crypto activity.

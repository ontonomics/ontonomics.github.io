Cryptocurrency - Vauld suspends withdrawals , trading and deposits

The cryptocurrency firm Vauld has suspended all withdrawals, trading and deposits on its platform with immediate effect following the collapse of Terraform Lab's Collateralised Loans scheme in July 2022, it has confirmed. Financial advice is being sought by the firm.

Paraguay new bill may turn the country into mining heaven

Paraguay has passed a bill which allows crypto miners to use excess energy from its two dams, Usina and Itaipu. The bill is expected to be approved by President Nicolas Maduro on 1 July.The new bill has been published on Twitter.

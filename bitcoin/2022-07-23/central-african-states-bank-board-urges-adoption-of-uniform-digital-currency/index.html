Central African States Bank Board Urges Adoption of Uniform Digital Currency

The Bank of Central African States has asked the central bank to introduce a digital currency for the region, according to the Bloomberg news agency Financial Times and the Reuters newspaper reports. They have also called on the Central Bank to adopt Bitcoin as legal tender.

Spanish market regulator warns about cryptocurrency event

Spanish stock market regulators have warned that a major cryptocurrency event is being held without financial permits, amid concerns about the risks of raising funds and providing investment services for teenagers and young people. Financial officials say they are concerned that the event could be illegal.

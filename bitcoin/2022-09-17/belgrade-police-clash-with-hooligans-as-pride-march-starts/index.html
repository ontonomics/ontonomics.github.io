Belgrade police clash with hooligans as Pride march starts

Serbian police have clashed with ultranationalist supporters at a Pride parade in Belgrade, where the event started despite threats from anti-gay groups and an official earlier ban, the Associated Press has learned. They say they have received guarantees that the events could go ahead.

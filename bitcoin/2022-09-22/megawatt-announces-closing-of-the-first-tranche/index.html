MegaWatt Announces Closing of The First Tranche

The company behind MegaWatt Lithium and battery metals has announced a closure of the first tranche of its non-brokered private placement (NFT) units. The announcement has been published by the Financial Conduct Authority (SEC) in Vancouver.

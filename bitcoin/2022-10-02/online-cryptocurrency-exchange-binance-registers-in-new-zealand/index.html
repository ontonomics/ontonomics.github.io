Online cryptocurrency exchange Binance registers in New Zealand

New Zealand s financial services provider Binance has registered with the Ministry of Business, Innovation and Employment, to help shape future regulation for the cryptocurrencies and non-fungible tokens industry, it has been revealed. However, the company says it does not have to comply with local regulations.

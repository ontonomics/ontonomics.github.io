Russia Supplying Kazakhstan with Electricity for Cryptocurrency Miners

Russia is planning to provide electricity to Kazakhstan's cryptocurrency mining operations as part of a move to boost energy supplies between the two bordered countries. The Russian government says it is considering changing the binational partnership with the former Soviet republic.

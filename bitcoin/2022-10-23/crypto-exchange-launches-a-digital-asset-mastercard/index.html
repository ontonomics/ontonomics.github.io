Crypto exchange launches a digital asset Mastercard

Australia’s largest cryptocurrency exchange, Coin Spot, has launched a digital currency card which allows users to spend their crypto currencies on the exchange in the country and around the world, according to its chief executive. Australians will be able to use the digital payments via the app.

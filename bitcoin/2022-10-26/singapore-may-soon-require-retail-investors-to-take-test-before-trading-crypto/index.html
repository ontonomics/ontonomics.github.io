Singapore may soon require retail investors to take test before trading crypto

Singapore’s National Bank (MAS) has proposed stricter measures to protect retail financial backers from the dangers of exchanging digital currency tokens. They could lead to higher risks being faced by crypto firms who have been willing to loan them.

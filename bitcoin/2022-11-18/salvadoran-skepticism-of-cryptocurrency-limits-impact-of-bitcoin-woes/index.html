Salvadoran skepticism of cryptocurrency limits impact of bitcoin woes

El Salvadorans have felt little impact because hardly anyone in the Central American country shares President Nayib Bukeles enthusiasm for cryptocurrency, according to an economist in San Salvador. While the government has lost millions of dollars amid the steep decline of the value of bitcoin, it is not talking about an economic catastrophe.

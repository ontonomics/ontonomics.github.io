Regine Gilbert , author of Inclusive Design for a Digital World : Designing with Accessibility in Mind , is appointed an NYU James Weldon Johnson Professor

The New York University (NYU) has named its first black professor as a James Weldon Johnson professor for the prestigious honorary professorship of the University of Ulster (UCL) in the New Year’s Honours list. Each professor has been awarded $25,000 per year.

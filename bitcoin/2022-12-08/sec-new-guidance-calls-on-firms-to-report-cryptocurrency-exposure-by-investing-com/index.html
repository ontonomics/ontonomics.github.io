SEC new guidance calls on firms to report cryptocurrency exposure By Investing . com

Companies issuing securities will now have to disclose their exposure to cryptocurrencies in public filings, the Securities and Exchange Commission (SEC) has announced, following the collapse of the crypto brokerage FTX bankruptcy and the loss of a digital-asset firm.

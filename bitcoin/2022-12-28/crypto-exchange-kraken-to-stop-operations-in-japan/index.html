Crypto exchange Kraken to stop operations in Japan

Kraken has announced plans to close its operations in Japan and de register from the financial services agency as part of its efforts to prioritise resources and investments in those areas that align with their strategy. The company has said it will withdraw its client service through Payward Asia as of January 31, 2023.

Indonesia to set up cryptocurrency exchange to reform crypto regulation

Indonesia has become the first country in the world to introduce a national cryptocurrency exchange, according to the country's financial services authority. The virtual rupiah could be available for sale in October, the Bank of Indonesia (BoI) says.

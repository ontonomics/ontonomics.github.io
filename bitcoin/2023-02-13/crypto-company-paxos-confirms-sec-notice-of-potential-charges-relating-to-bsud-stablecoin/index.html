Crypto company Paxos confirms SEC notice of potential charges relating to BSUD stablecoin

The US Securities and Exchange Commission (SEC) is considering recommending an action against Paxos over its failure to register a stablecoin called BUSD as an illegal security, it has been revealed. However, the company says it is prepared to vigorously litigate if necessary.

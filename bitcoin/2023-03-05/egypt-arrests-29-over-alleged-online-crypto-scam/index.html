Egypt arrests 29 over alleged online crypto scam

Egyptian authorities have detained 29 people, including 13 foreigners, accused of running an online cryptocurrency scam that defrauded thousands of investors, state media reported on Sunday. They ran a scam which cost millions of Egyptians more than $620,000.

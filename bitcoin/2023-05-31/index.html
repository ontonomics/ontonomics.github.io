In other news, Pico, a global provider of technology services for the financial markets, has introduced its Corvil Cloud Analytics solution for crypto trading environments. With its proven track record in the traditional finance market, Corvil Cloud Analytics aims to provide the same level of visibility for crypto trading businesses. The partnership between Idex and AuthenTrend highlights the increasing importance of biometrics in securing digital assets and identities.

In regulatory news, the proposed “Crypto Regulation, Protection, Transparency, and Oversight (CRPTO) Act” in New York aims to impose stricter regulations on digital currency businesses operating in or from the state. The bill seeks to give the New York Department of Financial Services (DFS) more authority to oversee virtual currency businesses and grant the Attorney General (AG) expanded enforcement powers. If enacted, the law would require digital asset brokers, marketplaces, advisers, and issuers to register with the New York Department of Law and comply with various requirements. These include posting audited financial statements and minimizing conflicts of interest. The bill also addresses customer liability, trade practice violations, and the obligations of digital asset influencers. This proposed legislation reflects the growing need for regulatory oversight in the crypto industry to protect investors and ensure transparency.

Bruce Springsteen , Shania Twain to honor Jon Bon Jovi at Grammy MusiCares Person of the Year gala

Jon Bon Jovi, Bruce Springsteen and Shania Twain have been confirmed as presenters at a music festival in Los Angeles, the US state of New Jersey, for their work offstage to help those who are in need of financial assistance during the coronavirus pandemic. They are among the stars who will be the headliner of this years Musicares. But The BBC Newsnight has revealed the details of what will happen in the event ahead of the 2021 Grammy Awards - and what is expected to be known as the Person of The Year celebrations which will take place in February next year. The singer is to appear at an event that will raise $39.5m (27m) to fund emergency funding for the music industry relief effort to tackle the impact of Covid-19 on the world s most successful music legends to take part in an annual benefit event in California, US and Canada, to mark the end of 2020, and where they are to perform at the ceremony in 2021, as part of an award-winning music giant raising funds to support millions of people who have died from the virus and the deaths of more than 50 million people across the country.

Lionsgate Network Investigates UNRWA's Digital Wallets Amid Terrorism Concerns

Published: 2024-02-02

A company called Lionsgate Network helped the Israeli government get back $90 million in Hamas cryptocurrency after an attack. Now, they are looking into the digital wallets of a group called UNRWA, which might be supporting terrorists. Lionsgate Network wants to check a UNRWA charity in the US that accepts cryptocurrency donations. They want to make sure the money is secure and not going to bad people.

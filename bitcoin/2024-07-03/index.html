Recently, SingularityNET (AGIX) saw a 1.1% drop in value against the U.S. dollar. Its market value is now $735.92 million. Over the past week, AGIX traded 16.1% lower. Other cryptocurrencies like Toncoin (TON) and Axie Infinity (AXS) also saw drops. SingularityNET has a total of 1,376,451,174 tokens and can be traded on many exchanges. Investors often buy Bitcoin or Ethereum first on platforms like Changelly or Coinbase to get SingularityNET.

SafeMoon (SAFEMOON) has kept a stable value against the U.S. dollar. Its market value is $2.80 million. SafeMoon has special features like Reflection, LP Acquisition, and Burn, which aim to increase earnings for token holders. SafeMoon plans to launch an NFT exchange, charity projects, and educational apps. It also wants to start a decentralized exchange and expand partnerships. Investors usually buy Ethereum or Bitcoin first, then trade for SafeMoon on specific exchanges.

Mask Network (MASK) saw a 4.6% drop in value against the USD on July 3rd, with one token now priced at $2.43. In the past week, it traded 3.2% lower. Its market value is $243.45 million. Mask Network is a decentralized app that connects web 2.0 and web 3.0. It offers access to DeFi, secure file sharing, encrypted messaging, and NFT creation. Investors often buy Bitcoin or Ethereum first to get MASK.

MagnetGold (MTG) saw a 1.4% increase in value against the U.S. dollar. Its market value is $22.96 million. One token is priced at $0.0842 or 0.00000140 BTC. Over the past week, MagnetGold traded 9.7% higher. MagnetGold is a decentralized cryptocurrency on the Binance Smart Chain and has a total of 700,000,000 tokens. Investors usually buy Ethereum or Bitcoin first on platforms like Gemini or Coinbase to get MagnetGold.

Jet Protocol (JET) saw no change in value against the U.S. dollar in the last 24 hours. Its market value is $2.62 million. One token can be bought for $0.0015 or 0.00000003 BTC. Jet Protocol was launched in 2021 on the Solana platform and has a total of 1,700,000,000 tokens. Investors often buy Ethereum or Bitcoin first on platforms like Gemini or GDAX, then trade for JET on supported exchanges.

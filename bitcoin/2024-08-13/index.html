"Blockchain Innovations and Market Trends: A Comprehensive Crypto Update"

Published: 2024-08-13

Welcome to today’s edition of our Daily Bitcoin Digest. We bring you the latest news and insights about cryptocurrency. Whether you are an experienced investor or just starting, we’ve got something for you.

Essential, a blockchain company, has raised $11 million in Series A funding led by Archetype. This funding comes when blockchain and crypto investments are generally down. Essential wants to make user experiences better by focusing on “intent-centric” services. They manage user actions off-chain with special tools. This helps with scalability and reduces on-chain processing.

Neblio (NEBL) has dropped 1.2% in value against the US dollar, with a market cap of $55,980.56. Over the past week, Neblio’s value fell by 67.5%. Quantfury Token (QTF) also saw a 4.1% decrease, trading at $5.34. Despite these drops, other cryptocurrencies like Kitty Inu (KITTY) and MVP Coin (MVP) have shown gains.

A recent podcast talks about the rise and fall of cryptocurrency during the pandemic. It focuses on key figures like Sam Bankman-Fried. The podcast explores the impact of NFTs, ethical issues, and how crypto is similar to the traditional financial system. It also discusses the 2022 crypto crash and compares it to the 2008 financial crisis.

The XRP options market on Deribit is getting more attention, especially the $1.10 call option. Experts see this as a positive sign, possibly due to speculation about an XRP ETF approval in the U.S. Some traders doubt XRP will reach $1.10 by the end of August because the timeframe is tight.

Valhalla’s PlayToEarn MMORPG will be featured in a big advertising campaign during the 2024-25 English Premier League season. The campaign aims to reach over 200 million people, generating 1.2 billion impressions globally. Valhalla offers a unique gaming experience inspired by Norse mythology, supported by the Floki cryptocurrency.

ARAW (ARAW) saw a 0.3% increase in trading against the US dollar, with a market cap of $2.06 billion. Ren (REN) experienced a 3.8% increase, with a market cap of $38.19 million. Theta Fuel (TFUEL) saw a 5.8% increase, with a market cap of $390.90 million. These positive trends show growing interest and confidence in these digital currencies.

Rainbow Rumble, a PvP skill-based game by Laguna Games, is now live on the Xai Network. Players compete in obstacle courses with Unicorns to earn Crypto Unicorns token ($CU) and rewards. The game offers various modes and is part of the Xai Vanguard:Genesis campaign, offering rewards like USDC, $CU, NFTs, and more.

SpritzMoon Crypto Token (Spritzmoon) saw a slight increase in trading value, with a market cap of $1.00 million. CV SHOTS experienced a slight decrease in trading value, with a market cap of $1.78 million. Walken (WLKN) experienced a 1.7% decrease in value, with a market cap of $1.48 million. UnlimitedIP (UIP) experienced a 0.8% decrease in trading, with a market cap of $16.65 million. DeFi Technologies Inc. will hold a shareholder call on August 15, 2024, to discuss its financial performance. PlayDapp (PLA) saw a slight increase in trading value, with a market cap of $27.76 million. BABB (BAX) saw a 1.6% increase in trading, with a market cap of $4.95 million. LUKSO (LYXe) saw a 16% increase in trading, with a market cap of $33.38 million. UNUS SED LEO (LEO) experienced slight changes, with a market cap of $5.36 billion.

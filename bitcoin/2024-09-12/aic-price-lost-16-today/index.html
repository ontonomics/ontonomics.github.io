AIC Price Lost 16 % Today

The AI Companions (AIC) token experienced a significant price fluctuation after its listing on crypto exchanges on September 11. Initially, the AIC price was $0.040, but it quickly rose to an all-time high (ATH) of $0.08177 on the first day of trading. The token price surged by approximately 117% on the first day. However, the following day, the AIC price sharply collapsed to an all-time low (ATL) of $0.062. As of the latest update, the AIC token is valued at $0.064. Many AI tokens, including NEAR Protocol, Artificial Superintelligence Alliance (FET), and Ocean Protocol, have shown positive growth in the last 24 hours. The Internet Computer (ICP) and Bittensor (TAO) have had minimal reactions. The AIC token is currently traded on three centralized exchanges: BingX, MEXC, and BitMart, with BingX being the largest exchange by volume of AIC. The future of the AIC price remains uncertain due to limited data. However, some traders, like Crypto Bullet on X, believe that the AIC token has the potential to reach the important $0.1 level, as it has shown bullish sentiment after its listing.

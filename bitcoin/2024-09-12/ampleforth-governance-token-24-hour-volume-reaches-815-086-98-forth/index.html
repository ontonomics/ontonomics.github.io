Ampleforth Governance Token 24 Hour Volume Reaches $815 , 086 . 98 ( FORTH )

Ampleforth Governance Token (FORTH) experienced a 2.3% increase in value against the dollar on September 12th, with one token priced at $2.98 or 0.00005145 BTC. Over the past week, FORTH has seen a 11.1% gain. The total market capitalization stands at $42.46 million, with $815,086.98 worth of tokens traded on exchanges in the last day. Related cryptocurrencies had mixed performances, with Aidi Finance (AIDI) and MVP Coin (MVP) trading down by 2.2%, while Lumi Credits (LUMI) saw an 8.9% increase.

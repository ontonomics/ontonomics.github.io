Apex Legends Announces All - New Space Hunt Event

Apex Legends, developed by Respawn Entertainment and published by Electronic Arts, is set to introduce a new sci-fi themed event called Space Hunt, starting on September 17. The event will feature new activities and collectibles, including 36 bounty hunter-themed cosmetics and an all-new melee cosmetic called Serpent s Sting. Players will participate in the Knockout LTM, where they must capture zones and score points to avoid elimination. Additionally, players can complete weekly Amped Knockout Trials to unlock Legends Crypto and Wattson, earning an Epic badge for completing both sets of unique Legend challenges. Throughout the Space Hunt event, players can earn Bounty Bucks for items in the Reward Shop, including two Epic+ Skins for the Havoc and Prowler. The 36 cosmetics will be unlocked as players progress, bringing them closer to Milestone Rewards. The event will run until October 8, with Duos returning for the remainder of the season.

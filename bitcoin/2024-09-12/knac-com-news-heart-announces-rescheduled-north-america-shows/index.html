KNAC . COM - News - HEART Announces Rescheduled North America Shows

HEART, the legendary rock band, has announced a series of rescheduled shows for 2025 after their successful Spring performances. The band will perform in 19 North American cities, starting with a show at the Fontainebleau in Las Vegas on February 28. The announcement came after the band had to postpone their 2024 dates due to health reasons. Ann Wilson expressed excitement about the band s return, stating, The best is yet to come! Nancy Wilson also shared her enthusiasm, saying, We are so excited to resume this tour. The band has a history spanning five decades, selling over 35 million albums worldwide and having 20 Top 40 singles. Their 2025 tour will feature classic hits like Magic Man, Barracuda, Crazy on You, and These Dreams. The current lineup of HEART includes Nancy Wilson, Ann Wilson, Ryan Wariner, Ryan Waters, Paul Moak, Tony Lucido, and Sean T. Lane. Tickets for the rescheduled shows are now available on the band s official website, with previously purchased tickets honored for the new dates. The first show of the 2025 Royal Flush Tour will take place in Las Vegas on February 28 at the Fontainebleau. Additional dates will be announced as they are confirmed. The band also offers exclusive VIP Packages, which include reserved seated tickets, custom merchandise, and more. For more information and updates on future dates, fans can visit the band s website at www.heart-music.com.

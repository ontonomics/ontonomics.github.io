Optimism ( OP ) Price Tops $1 . 54

Optimism (OP) experienced a 1% decrease in value against the dollar on September 12th, despite a 14% increase over the previous seven days. With a market capitalization of $1.82 billion, $120.46 million worth of OP was traded on exchanges on the last day. The current price of one OP token is approximately $1.54 or 0.00002642 BTC. Similar cryptocurrencies also saw fluctuations in value against the dollar on the same day. Aidi Finance (AIDI) and Zoo Token (ZOOT) both decreased by 2.2%, while Hokkaidu Inu (HOKK) increased by 1.2%. Jeff in Space (JEFF) and MVP Coin (MVP) both decreased by 2.2% and 3.4%, respectively. Optimism was launched on November 11th, 2021, with a total supply of 4,294,967,296 tokens and a circulating supply of 1,187,596,466 tokens. The official platforms for Optimism include optimism.mirror.xyz, @optimism on Twitter, and www.optimism.io. To trade OP, investors must first acquire Ethereum or Bitcoin using a U.S. dollar-based exchange like Coinbase, Gemini, or Changelly. They can then use their Ethereum or Bitcoin to purchase OP on the same exchanges.

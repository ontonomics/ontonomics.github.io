Tectum Wins the Best Blockchain Innovation Award at the Leaders In Fintech Awards 2024

Tectum, a subsidiary of Crispmind, has been honored with the Best Blockchain Innovation award at the Leaders in Fintech Awards 2024. This recognition highlights Tectum as a leading blockchain service provider in the MENA region. The company, known for its fastest layer-1 blockchain with a transaction speed of over 3.5 million transactions per second, is celebrated for its innovative solutions in the fintech industry. Tectum s ecosystem includes SoftNote, a fintech solution that scales Bitcoin transactions, and SoftNote Wallet, a user-friendly wallet for managing digital assets. Additionally, SoftNote Bills and SoftNote Pay offer crypto cash systems and direct crypto payments, respectively. The Leaders in Fintech Awards 2024, held at the Habtoor Palace in Dubai, features 38 categories that acknowledge the impact of individuals and companies in the financial technology sector. The event encourages participation from all service providers in the MENA region, with voting open from the nominations announcement until September 3rd. Tectum s success in the award showcases its commitment to creating efficient and user-friendly blockchain solutions. The company s diverse portfolio, including secure messaging, user authentication, and cryptocurrency, demonstrates its dedication to addressing complex challenges in the digital age.

Trump plans to launch his son crypto business on Monday , 50 days before Election Day – Winnipeg Free Press

Former President Donald Trump plans to address cryptocurrency and his new venture, World Liberty Financial, a crypto platform controlled by his sons Donald Jr. and Eric. Trump s speech is scheduled for 50 days before the upcoming Election Day, marking a significant use of his remaining campaign time to promote a personal business. Throughout his 2024 campaign, Trump has intertwined his political and business interests, marketing various products including sneakers, photo books, and Trump-branded Bibles. In a recent video, Trump expressed his support for cryptocurrencies, stating that the United States should embrace this future technology and move away from traditional big banks. The Winnipeg Free Press reports that cryptocurrencies are digital forms of money that can be traded online without relying on the global banking system. However, Trump s stance on cryptocurrencies has evolved over time. During his presidency, he opposed the sector, but has since shown support for it. Trump has suggested that the government create a strategic reserve of Bitcoin and has vowed to block the creation of a Central Bank Digital Currency (CBDC), a digital form of central bank money available to the public. This shift in Trump s position on cryptocurrencies has raised concerns about potential conflicts of interest, as he could use the federal government to support a business tied to his family.

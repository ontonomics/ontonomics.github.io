"Daily Crypto Market Highlights: Gains for iExec RLC, Compound, and More"

Published: 2024-09-13

Welcome to today’s Bitcoin Digest! We’re here to break down the latest news in the world of cryptocurrency. Whether you’re an experienced investor or just curious, we’ve got the most interesting updates for you.

In the last 24 hours, some cryptocurrencies have done well. iExec RLC (RLC) went up by 1.4% against the US dollar. Its market cap is now $110.16 million. Over the past week, it rose by 11.3% and is now priced at $1.52 or 0.00002513 BTC. iExec RLC runs on the Ethereum platform and can be traded using Bitcoin or Ethereum.

Compound (COMP) also went up by 1.6%, trading at about $44.82 or 0.00074022 BTC. Its market cap is $388.96 million, and it increased by 9.9% over the past week. This token can be traded on platforms like compoundlabs.xyz.

Prometeus (PROM) increased by 2%, trading at $5.32 or 0.00008817 BTC. With a market cap of $102.32 million, Prometeus supports industries like influencer marketing and insurance. It can be bought using Ethereum or Bitcoin on exchanges like GDAX, Coinbase, or Gemini.

Decentraland (MANA) saw a 2.1% increase, with a market cap of $536.57 million. Priced at $0.28, Decentraland has risen by 13.5% over the past week. This digital asset on the Ethereum blockchain is used for virtual land transactions and can be traded on exchanges like GDAX, Gemini, or Coinbase.

Metal (MTL) also went up by 2%, with a market cap of $61.85 million and a price of $0.93 per token. Metal powers the Metal Pay digital wallet, offering fast transactions and up to 5% cashback on purchases.

Not all cryptocurrencies had a good day. UnlimitedIP (UIP) decreased by 0.8%, with a market cap of $16.65 million. Priced at $0.0099 or 0.00000016 BTC, it can be traded using Ethereum or Bitcoin on exchanges like Gemini, GDAX, or Changelly.

BABB (BAX) saw a 1% decrease over the past 24 hours but increased by 12.2% over the past week. With a market cap of $4.43 million, BABB powers a decentralized banking platform aimed at the microeconomy, offering UK bank accounts via a smartphone app.

In legal news, Sam Bankman-Fried, founder of the bankrupt crypto exchange FTX, was convicted of fraud, conspiracy, and money laundering. He got a 25-year prison sentence. His defense argues that the trial had errors and was rushed, seeking a new trial.

On the corporate front, MicroStrategy shares surged 8% on Friday and 24% for the week after founder Michael Saylor announced a $1.1 billion bitcoin purchase. This brings their total to 244,800 bitcoins worth $14.6 billion. MicroStrategy aims to bridge traditional investors with cryptocurrency, highlighting a 17% “BTC yield” for the year.

Euler (EUL) increased by 2.8% against the dollar, with a market cap of $74.19 million. Priced at $4.45, Euler is a DeFi protocol on Ethereum, enabling lending and borrowing of digital assets.

Banxa Holdings Inc. (CVE:BNXA) hit a new 52-week low at C$0.33, closing at C$0.34. The company provides payment services for cryptocurrency exchanges, including Web3 on-and-off ramp solutions.

The world of cryptocurrency is always changing with daily ups and downs. Whether you’re looking to invest or just stay informed, understanding these digital assets and their technologies is important. Stay tuned for more updates and insights as we continue to explore this exciting digital world together.

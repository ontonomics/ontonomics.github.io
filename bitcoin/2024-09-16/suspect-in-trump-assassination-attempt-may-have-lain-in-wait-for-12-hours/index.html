Suspect in Trump assassination attempt may have lain in wait for 12 hours

Ryan W Routh, a 58-year-old man with prior felony convictions, has been charged with two gun-related crimes after being spotted with a rifle near former President Donald Trump s golf course in Florida. Phone records suggest Routh may have been waiting for nearly 12 hours, hiding in the bushes with a loaded SKS semi-automatic rifle. Despite being arrested and held on charges of illegally possessing a firearm with an obliterated serial number, Routh did not fire any shots and never had Trump in his line of sight. The FBI is investigating the incident as an assassination attempt on Trump, with the US Attorney for the Southern District of Florida, Markenzy Lapointe, confirming the seriousness of the case. Routh s arrest followed a failed attempt on Trump s life during a July rally in Pennsylvania, raising concerns about the violent nature of US politics. Routh s social media posts are being examined for clues regarding his alleged planning of the crime and his motive. He has expressed support for Ukraine and has been a staunch Trump supporter, but his recent posts have shown support for Biden and Harris. Routh moved to Hawaii in 2018 and has a history of felony convictions in North Carolina. The Secret Service, which protects US presidents and presidential candidates, has been under scrutiny since the botched handling of the July attempt on Trump s life. Trump has thanked the Secret Service and law enforcement for keeping him safe, while condemning President Joe Biden and Vice President Kamala Harris for the apparent assassination attempt. Biden and Harris have condemned political violence and directed their team to ensure the Secret Service has every resource necessary to protect Trump. Routh s connection to Ukraine and his social media posts supporting Ukraine and describing Trump as a threat to US democracy have been noted. However, Ukrainian officials and The International Legion have distanced themselves from Routh. The investigation into Routh s alleged assassination attempt continues.

Nervos Network ( CKB ) 24 - Hour Trading Volume Reaches $283 . 51 Million

Nervos Network (CKB) experienced a 5.9% increase in value against the U.S. dollar in a 24-hour period ending September 17th, with a market capitalization of $743.35 million and $283.51 million worth of Nervos Network traded on exchanges. In the past week, the cryptocurrency has seen an 88.6% gain against the dollar. Related cryptocurrencies also showed varied performance, with Bitcoin (BTC) up 3.5%, Dogecoin (DOGE) up 1.9%, Bitcoin Cash (BCH) up 1%, UNUS SED LEO (LEO) down 0.5%, Litecoin (LTC) up 1.8%, Monero (XMR) up 0.7%, Ethereum Classic (ETC) up 1.9%, Arweave (AR) down 1.3%, and Bitcoin SV (BSV) up 2.7%. Nervos Network, a proof-of-work (PoW) coin using the Eaglesong hashing algorithm, was first traded on November 15th, 2019. The total supply is 45,674,063,894 coins, with a circulating supply of 44,955,510,964 coins. The CKB token serves as the foundational layer of the Nervos Network s public blockchain ecosystem, used for state storage, resource management, and network incentives. To acquire Nervos Network, investors must first purchase Ethereum or Bitcoin using a U.S. dollar-based exchange like Coinbase, Gemini, or Changelly. They can then use their newly-acquired cryptocurrency to buy Nervos Network on the same exchanges.

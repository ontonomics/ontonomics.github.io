Trump Passes Out Hamburgers at New York Bitcoin Bar Ahead of Rally

Former President Donald Trump was spotted distributing hamburgers at a Bitcoin-themed bar in Manhattan, New York City, prior to his Uniondale rally. The video, shared by Trump s deputy communications director Margo Martin, showed Trump offering burgers to the crowd and referring to them as Bitcoin and a Crypto burgers. The footage also captured Trump making a public transaction using cryptocurrency at the bar, marking the first instance of a president engaging in such a transaction. Trump, who has expressed support for Bitcoin and other cryptocurrencies, has previously declared his intention to be a crypto president. He announced his new cryptocurrency venture, World Liberty Financial, during an interview on X Space. Trump is scheduled to hold a rally at the Nassau Veterans Memorial Coliseum at 7:00 p.m. EST.

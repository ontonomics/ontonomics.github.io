Miami man among 2 charged in $230 million cryptocurrency theft – NBC 6 South Florida

Miami man Malone Lam and California man Jeandiel Serrano are facing charges for a cryptocurrency scam that allegedly involved stealing and laundering over $230 million. The FBI arrested Lam and Serrano, who are known online as Anne Hathaway and $$ , and VersaceGod and @SkidStar , respectively. The scam, which began in August, involved the fraudulent access to victims cryptocurrency accounts and transferring the funds into the perpetrators possession. The stolen cryptocurrency was laundered through various mixers and exchanges using techniques like peel chains, pass-through wallets, and VPNs to conceal their identities. In one instance, Lam, Serrano, and their associates fraudulently obtained over 4,100 Bitcoin, worth more than $230 million at the time. The laundered funds were used for international travel, nightclub parties, luxury cars, watches, jewelry, designer handbags, and rental homes in Los Angeles and Miami. The investigation into the scam is ongoing, and authorities have not yet determined the full extent of the fraudulent activities or the total amount of money involved.

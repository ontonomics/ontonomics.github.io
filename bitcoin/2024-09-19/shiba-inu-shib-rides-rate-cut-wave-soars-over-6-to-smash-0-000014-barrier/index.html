Shiba Inu ( SHIB ) Rides Rate Cut Wave , Soars Over 6 % to Smash $0 . 000014 Barrier

Shiba Inu (SHIB) experienced a significant surge of over 6% in the past 24 hours, reaching a price of $0.000014. This increase is attributed to a recent rate cut that sparked a market-wide rally. The trading volume of SHIB also rose by more than 60%, reaching $216,950,085, indicating investor optimism and potential for further price growth. The meme coin s metrics have shown considerable improvement from their low points, with daily active addresses and other key network measures also improving. This positive trend suggests that SHIB could continue its upward trajectory, potentially reaching the $0.000015 region if the current momentum persists. The internal ecosystem growth of SHIB may also play a role in its price recovery. Despite a record-low burn rate, previous token flames have restored faith in the ecosystem s ability to deflate the massive supply. This, combined with the current positive trend, could lead to a stronger comeback in SHIB s price. Overall, the recent developments in SHIB s trading volume, network measures, and ecosystem growth have contributed to its impressive price surge. The market s renewed optimism and the potential for further growth make SHIB a key player in the cryptocurrency revival.

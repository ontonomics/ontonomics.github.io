Shiba Inu Shytoshi Kusama Teases Tron Partnership at Token2049 , SHIB Army Ablaze

Shytoshi Kusama, the leader of the Shiba Inu (SHIB) community, sparked excitement and speculation with a mysterious tweet during his appearance at the Token2049 blockchain event in Singapore. The tweet featured a photo of Kusama wearing a white mask, similar to the one he wore during his meet and greet tour in Japan and India earlier this year. The tweet also included a Tron DAO card with the Tron blockchain logo, leading to rumors of a potential partnership between SHIB and Tron. Justin Sun, the founder of cryptocurrency Tron, was present at the event as a sponsor. Kusama had previously met with the SHIB army at the Web3 IVS 2024 conference in Kyoto, where he wore a katana, hood, and two masks. The SHIB community has been buzzing with anticipation, as the price of Shiba Inu surged by 9.35% in the past 24 hours, reaching $0.00001424. The speculation surrounding the potential partnership between SHIB and Tron comes amidst a broader context of interest rate discussions. Jerome Powell, the Fed chairman, mentioned a possible 50-basis-point interest rate drop during the FOMC meeting, marking the first such drop since 2020. In summary, Shytoshi Kusama s mysterious tweet and presence at the Token2049 event have fueled excitement and speculation within the Shiba Inu community, with potential partnership rumors with Tron DAO and a significant price surge for the cryptocurrency. The broader context of interest rate discussions adds to the overall market sentiment.

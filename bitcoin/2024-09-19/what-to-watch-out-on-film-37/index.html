What to Watch : Out On Film 37

Mother Father Sister Brother Frank is a dramatic film that intensifies relatable family tensions with elements of money laundering, blackmail, and murder, while also delivering humor. Mindy Cohn s performance as Joy is highlighted, with the entire cast s comedic talents praised. Becoming a Man in 127 Easy Steps is a unique film that explores trans identity through a mix of stand-up comedy, animation, and personal stories. Cashing Out is a concise documentary that delves into the viatical settlement industry during the AIDS crisis, offering insight into the complexities of life insurance sales to terminally ill patients. Under the Influencer presents themes of digital exploitation and mental health, despite a confusing ending. Big Easy Queens is a campy musical with memorable performances and a humorous death scene. Maxxie LaWow: Drag Queen Super-Shero is a kid-friendly film with adorable animation and a heartwarming story. Striking with Pride: United at the Coalface is a compelling documentary about the unlikely alliance between gay men and miners in the 1980s. Ella is a slow-burning film that fails to engage due to its lack of plot and bizarre cinematic choices. Lady Like is a documentary about Lady Camden from RuPaul s Drag Race, but it lacks narrative structure and originality.

Zach Bryan apologizes for stupid Kanye West - Taylor Swift tweet

Country music artist Zach Bryan recently apologized for a controversial tweet comparing Taylor Swift to Kanye West, which sparked outrage among Swift fans and the public. The tweet, which compared Swift to West and the Kansas City Chiefs, was deleted after it went viral. Bryan, a 28-year-old Country Music Association nominee, later clarified that he was under the influence when he posted the tweet and was not targeting Taylor. He expressed his love for Swift s music and admitted to often tweeting foolish things. Bryan shared that he was going through personal struggles at the time and was projecting a bit in his tweet. He also acknowledged the ongoing feud between Swift and West, stating that his post came off as rude and desensitized. Bryan praised Swift s contributions to the music industry and emphasized that he has no malice or meanness towards anyone. In his Instagram apology, Bryan stated that he intends to focus on himself, including wrapping up his Quittin Time tour. He also mentioned that he was nominated for the 2024 CMA Awards for his collaboration with Kacey Musgraves. Bryan s representatives did not immediately respond to a request for additional comment.

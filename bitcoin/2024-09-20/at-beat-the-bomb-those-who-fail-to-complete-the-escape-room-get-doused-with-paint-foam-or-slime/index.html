At Beat the Bomb , those who fail to complete the escape room get doused with paint , foam or slime

Beat The Bomb is a team-building experience that opens on Oct. 11 at 1218 Chestnut St. Participants, in groups of 4-6, must complete a series of objectives in four game rooms to disarm a bomb filled with paint, foam, or slime within an hour. The game rooms include Crypto Laser, Hack Attack, and Word Bank, each presenting unique challenges. Participants wear hazmat suits to protect their clothing. The success rate for the Paint Bomb Mission is 10%, while the Foam Bomb Mission has a 5% completion rate. The Slime Bomb Mission is a new addition in Philadelphia. Rewards for disarming the bomb include commemorative keepsakes and access to tournaments. After the intense experience, teams can relax at the Bomb Bar, which features a 1,500-square-foot beer garden. For those who prefer not to get splattered, there are Arcade Game Bays with collaborative and competitive mini-games for teams of 2-4 people. Tickets for the Beat The Bomb missions start at $39.95, while tickets for the Arcade Game Bays are $19.95. The experience is available in locations across Brooklyn, Washington, and Atlanta. Participants leave with photos and videos of their adventure.

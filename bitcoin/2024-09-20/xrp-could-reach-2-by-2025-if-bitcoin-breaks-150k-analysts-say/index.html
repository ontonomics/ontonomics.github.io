XRP Could Reach $2 by 2025 if Bitcoin Breaks $150K , Analysts Say

The cryptocurrency market is bullish, with Bitcoin expected to reach $100,000 to $150,000, and XRP potentially hitting $2 by 2025. The SEC s decision not to appeal the Ripple verdict and the anticipated Bitcoin price surge contribute to this optimism. The US Federal Reserve s interest rate cut has marked the start of a second bull market wave. Bitcoin s price has seen a 10% increase in the past week, with a 21% drop in trading volume indicating traders disinterest. Analysts predict a breakout from the cup-and-handle formation on the Bitcoin price chart could lead to a significant bullish rise. However, Bitcoin must first consolidate and break above $67K before reaching $100,000. CredibleCrypto expects XRP to surge 25% to $0.75, but it must first break below its lowest point before advancing upwards. The SEC s lack of intent to appeal the Ripple case and the upcoming elections are expected to improve traders moods. The combination of bullish Bitcoin momentum and regulatory clarity fuels optimism in the market. As a result, the price of XRP is expected to rise, potentially reaching $2 by 2025.

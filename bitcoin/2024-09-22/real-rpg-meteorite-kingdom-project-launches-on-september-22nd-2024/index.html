REAL RPG Meteorite Kingdom Project launches on September 22nd , 2024

The Meteorite Kingdom Project, a revolutionary REAL RPG platform, launches on September 22, 2024, in Bangkok, Thailand. It introduces a concept that intertwines gaming with real-world systems, suggesting that life is a game. This platform aims to address global issues such as wars, economic stagnation, and personal struggles by fostering human potential and innovation through blockchain technology. The platform spans multiple sectors, including art, finance, science and technology, medical care, sports, business, and human relations. It offers global access to art, music, and products, plans to list Meteorite Tokens and open MTT Bank in the United States, and develops quantum technology, wearable devices, and a mineral refinery. The project also aims to integrate Western and Oriental medicine in hospitals in Japan and Mongolia, provide unique financing solutions for businesses, and launch global sports events. The Meteorite Kingdom Project emphasizes the importance of integration and collective excitement in tackling loneliness and societal challenges. It encourages users to view life as a game, where success is measured by excitement and collaboration rather than traditional financial credit systems. The project has a ten-year vision, with updates and innovations planned for every sphere of human existence. The roadmap includes the development of devices such as watches and rings, construction of a mineral refinery, and the launch of global sports events. For more information, visit www.meteorite.io.

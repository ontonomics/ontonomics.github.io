Bitcoin Depot Inc . ( NASDAQ : BTM ) Major Shareholder Purchases $65 , 552 . 00 in Stock

Andrew Mitchell Paul, a major shareholder of Bitcoin Depot Inc. (NASDAQ:BTM), recently increased his stake in the company by purchasing 38,560 shares on August 12th at an average cost of $1.70 per share, totaling $65,552.00. This acquisition brought his total ownership to 155,786 shares, valued at $264,836.20. Paul s transactions also included purchases of 97,804 shares on August 9th and 19,422 shares on August 5th, at respective average costs of $1.66 and $1.54 per share. Bitcoin Depot s stock experienced a 3.8% decline on the day of Paul s latest purchase, reaching $1.53 per share. The company s market capitalization stands at $91.21 million, with a negative price-to-earnings ratio of -0.82 and a beta of -0.34. Institutional investors hold 9.18% of the company s stock, with several hedge funds making significant changes to their stakes. Bitcoin Depot Inc. operates a network of cryptocurrency kiosks in North America, allowing customers to buy and sell bitcoin, litecoin, and ethereum. The company also offers retail locations and an over-the-counter trade platform. Analysts from HC Wainwright have recently lowered their price objective for Bitcoin Depot from $4.00 to $3.50, maintaining a buy rating for the company.

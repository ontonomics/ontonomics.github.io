Waves Market Capitalization Reaches $131 . 97 Million ( WAVES )

Waves (WAVES), a LPoS cryptocurrency using the Leased POS hashing algorithm, experienced a 0.8% increase against the U.S. dollar in a one-day period ending September 23rd. The coin s value stands at approximately $1.14 or 0.00001803 BTC on major exchanges. Over the past week, Waves has seen a 12.5% gain against the U.S. dollar. With a market capitalization of $131.97 million, around $15 million worth of Waves was traded on exchanges on the previous day. Waves was launched on April 12th, 2016, and has a total supply of 115,736,632 coins. The official website (waves.tech) and social media platforms (Twitter @wavesprotocol, Facebook page) are available for users. The Reddit community (https://reddit.com/r/wavesplatform) and GitHub account (https://github.com/wavesplatform) also provide additional resources. According to CryptoCompare, Waves is an open blockchain protocol and development toolset for Web 3.0 applications and decentralized solutions, created by entrepreneur Sasha Ivanov in 2016. It aims to enhance security, reliability, and speed of IT systems, while simplifying the development and operation of decentralized applications (dApps). To trade Waves, investors must first acquire Ethereum or Bitcoin using a U.S. dollar-based exchange like GDAX, Gemini, or Coinbase. They can then use their Ethereum or Bitcoin to purchase Waves on the same exchanges.

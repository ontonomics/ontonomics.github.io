Newswire & Press Release / Group - IB and I . T Protective Security Services Join Hands to Strengthen Cybersecurity in Brunei Darussalam - IT Security / Anti - Spam / Cybersecurity - Group - IB

Group-IB, a prominent cybersecurity technology provider, has entered into a Memorandum of Understanding (MOU) with I.T Protective Security Services (ITPSS Sdn Bhd) to bolster Brunei s cyber resilience and safeguard its national interests and IT infrastructure. The partnership aims to enhance cybersecurity for Brunei s citizens and businesses through various initiatives, including sharing cyber threat intelligence, providing technical assistance for vulnerability assessments and digital forensics, and conducting training programs to improve cybersecurity awareness and incident response capabilities. The collaboration will also focus on promoting cybersecurity best practices among businesses and the public, while delivering essential cybersecurity services to Small-and-Medium Enterprises (SMEs) in Brunei. Group-IB s Digital Crime Resistance Centers (DCRCs) in the region will be utilized for rapid analysis and mitigation of regional threats, improving intelligence sharing, and facilitating joint operations against cybercrime within Brunei. The agreement was signed during the CySEC 2024 event in Brunei, between Shamsul Bahri Haji Kamis, CEO of ITPSS, and Dmitry Volkov, CEO of Group-IB. Founded in 2003 and headquartered in Singapore, Group-IB is a leading creator of cybersecurity technologies to investigate, prevent, and fight digital crime. The company s Digital Crime Resistance Centers (DCRCs) are strategically located in the Middle East, Europe, Central Asia, and Asia-Pacific to help analyze and mitigate regional and country-specific threats. Group-IB s Unified Risk Platform (URP) offers a comprehensive suite of cybersecurity solutions, including threat intelligence, fraud protection, AI-powered digital risk protection, multi-layered protection with Managed Extended Detection and Response (XDR), business email protection, and external attack surface management.

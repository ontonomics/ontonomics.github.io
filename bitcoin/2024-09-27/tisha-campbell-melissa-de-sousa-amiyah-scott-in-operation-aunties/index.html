Tisha Campbell , Melissa De Sousa , Amiyah Scott In Operation : Aunties

Operation: Aunties, an original feature film by TV network ALLBLK, stars Tisha Campbell, Melissa De Sousa, and Amayah Scott, directed by Wendy Raquel Robinson. The drama-comedy film follows the story of Dr. Carol Cleavers (De Sousa), a blockchain expert, and her retired detective best friend Sharon O Hara (Campbell), as they team up with their niece Aminah James (Scott) to combat a cyber-crime syndicate. The trio s mission exposes them to danger, while also addressing real societal issues. Brett Dismuke, Head of Content at ALLBLK, highlights the film s unique blend of action and comedy, showcasing the resilience, empowerment, and heroism of strong Black female characters. TJ Ali, writer and executive producer, emphasizes the importance of Black women as agents of change, and the significance of their representation in media. The film, which has yet to announce a premiere date, will be exclusively available on ALLBLK in 2025. The collaboration between ALLBLK, Wendy Raquel Robinson, Big Little Sis Media, and the talented cast aims to bring this empowering story to life, celebrating the role of Black women as vigilantes and heroes in society.

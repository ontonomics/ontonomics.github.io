Trump launches new business venture : $100 , 000 diamond - encrusted watches

Donald Trump, the Republican presidential candidate, has launched a new business venture with the unveiling of the Official Trump Watch Collection. The collection includes diamond-encrusted watches, with the most expensive model featuring 122 diamonds and priced at USD 100,000. Another model, the Fight Fight Fight, is available for USD 499. This latest venture follows Trump s tradition of merging his political and business interests, as he has previously sold Bibles, sneakers, photo books, and cryptocurrency during his third campaign for president. The launch of the watch collection comes 40 days before Election Day, potentially opening him up to criticism about monetizing his campaign. The websites for the various Trump-branded products note that proceeds from their sales do not directly benefit Trump or his campaign. However, they also mention that each product is subject to a paid license agreement. This mechanism has allowed Trump to profit from sales of various items, including water, vodka, steaks, and even digital trading cards featuring his cartoon-like images. The watches are covered by a similar agreement to license Trump s name, image, and likeness. The proceeds from their sales do not go to Trump s campaign or the Trump Organization. Instead, TheBestWatchesonEarth LLC, the sales entity, uses Trump s name under a paid license agreement, which may be terminated or revoked according to its terms. The watches are intended as collectible items for individual enjoyment only, not for investment purposes. The Trump campaign referred questions about the licensing deal to the Trump Organization, which did not immediately respond to a message seeking comment. TheBestWatchesonEarth LLC also did not immediately respond to an inquiry on the deal via its website. In summary, Donald Trump has launched a new business venture with diamond-encrusted watches, following his tradition of merging his political and business interests. The proceeds from the sales of these watches do not directly benefit Trump or his campaign, but are subject to a paid license agreement. The watches are intended as collectible items for individual enjoyment only.

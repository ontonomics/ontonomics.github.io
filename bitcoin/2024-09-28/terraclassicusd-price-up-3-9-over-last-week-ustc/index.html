TerraClassicUSD Price Up 3 . 9 % Over Last Week ( USTC )

TerraClassicUSD (USTC) experienced a 2.8% decline against the U.S. dollar on September 28th, following a 3.9% increase in the previous week. With a market capitalization of $108.15 million, $8.63 million worth of USTC was traded on exchanges on the last day. The current price of one USTC coin is approximately $0.0185 or 0.00000028 BTC. Other cryptocurrencies also saw fluctuations: Avalanche (AVAX) dropped 3.5% against the dollar, NEAR Protocol (NEAR) rose 1.2%, Internet Computer (ICP) fell 2.7%, Hedera (HBAR) decreased 1.3%, Cronos (CRO) dropped 1.9%, Cosmos (ATOM) fell 3.1%, Fetch.ai (FET) declined 5.8%, Algorand (ALGO) fell 2.7%, Akash Network (AKT) dropped 2%, and Theta Fuel (TFUEL) increased 1.4%. TerraClassicUSD, a digital asset on the Terra blockchain, was created after the de-pegging of TerraUSD (UST) from the U.S. dollar. It aims to maintain value relative to a basket of assets, including the U.S. Dollar and other stablecoins. TerraClassicUSD s total supply is 8,190,337,501 coins, with a circulating supply of 5,830,696,729 coins. To trade TerraClassicUSD, investors must first purchase Ethereum or Bitcoin using a U.S. dollar-based exchange like Changelly, GDAX, or Gemini. They can then use their acquired cryptocurrencies to purchase USTC on the same exchanges.

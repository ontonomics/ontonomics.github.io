Maker Tops One Day Trading Volume of $86 . 57 Million ( MKR )

Maker (MKR) experienced a 5.9% decline against the U.S. dollar in a 24-hour period ending October 1st, with a weekly drop of 7.3%. Related cryptocurrencies also saw fluctuations: Aidi Finance (AIDI) traded 2.2% lower against the dollar, Zoo Token (ZOOT) and CareCoin (CARES) both traded down 2.2%, Hokkaidu Inu (HOKK) traded 1.2% higher, Jeff in Space (JEFF) traded down 2.2%, and Lumi Credits (LUMI) traded up 8.9%. Maker was launched on August 15th, 2015, with a total supply of 909,177 tokens and a circulating supply of 878,456 tokens. The Maker community can be found on Reddit, GitHub, Twitter, and Facebook.

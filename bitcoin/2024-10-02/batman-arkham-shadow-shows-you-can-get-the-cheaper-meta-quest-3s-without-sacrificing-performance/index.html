Meta s Quest 3S headset, priced at $300, is positioned as a strong launch for the company, offering similar features to the Quest 3 at a lower cost. The Quest 3S is a gaming-centric device with a slightly reduced field of view (FOV) compared to the Quest 3, but it maintains good visual fidelity and hand tracking capabilities. The headset includes low-light sensors to compensate for the lack of a depth sensor. While the Quest 3S may not be a laptop replacement due to eye strain and limited battery life, it provides a cost-effective option for VR enthusiasts. The Quest 3S s performance and features make it a compelling choice for those looking to enter the virtual reality market.

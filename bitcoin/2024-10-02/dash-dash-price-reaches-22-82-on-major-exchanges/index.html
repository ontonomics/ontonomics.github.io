Dash ( DASH ) Price Reaches $22 . 82 on Major Exchanges

Dash (DASH) experienced a 1.3% increase against the U.S. dollar on October 2nd, with one Dash coin priced at approximately $22.82 or 0.00037564 BTC on major exchanges. Over the past week, Dash s value has decreased by 11.3% against the U.S. dollar. The cryptocurrency has a market cap of $273.28 million and $50.01 million worth of Dash was traded on exchanges on the previous day. Dash, a PoW/PoSe coin using the X11 hashing algorithm, was first traded on January 18th, 2014. With a total supply of 11,973,315 coins, Dash aims to be a user-friendly and scalable payments-focused cryptocurrency. It offers features like InstantSend, ChainLocks, and PrivateSend to enhance privacy and security. Dash s governance system, or treasury, distributes 10% of block rewards for project development in a decentralized manner. The Dash Foundation advocates for cryptocurrency adoption and receives donations and offers paid memberships. To acquire Dash, investors typically need to first purchase Ethereum or Bitcoin using a U.S. dollar-based exchange like Changelly, Coinbase, or GDAX. They can then use these cryptocurrencies to buy Dash on the same exchanges.

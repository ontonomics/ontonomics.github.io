Innovative Hydrogen Production Patent granted to Magma Power™ for Hydrogen production via Thermochemical Splitting with Subterranean Heat Sources and Magma Reservoirs
Magma Power LLC has issued a patent for a novel method and system that produces hydrogen through the thermochemical splitting of water, leveraging the heat from subterranean magma reservoirs. This technology represents a significant advancement in clean and sustainable hydrogen production, offering a new approach to harnessing geothermal energy for hydrogen generation. The system involves a wellbore that reaches a magma reservoir, where water is injected into a reaction chamber heated by molten salt from the wellbore. The process efficiently splits water into hydrogen and oxygen, with the potential for recycling unreacted water and integrating additional feed streams to enhance reactions. The patent claims include a thermochemical splitting system with a reaction chamber, inlet and outlet conduits, a return conduit for water recycling, and the option to integrate catalysts for optimized reactions. The system also features molten salt heat transfer, an absorption chiller and separator vessel, and downstream hydrogen utilization for hydrocarbon production. Levi Conner, CEO of Magma Power LLC, highlights the significance of this innovation, emphasizing its potential to reshape the energy landscape by providing a cleaner and more sustainable source of hydrogen. Richard McDonald, Chief Strategy Officer, points to the technological breakthroughs involved, such as molten salt heat transfer, catalyst-enhanced reactions, and water recycling, which contribute to the system s efficiency and environmental responsibility. KC Conner, Founder of Magma Power LLC, reflects on the company s mission to harness the Earth s energy in innovative ways, with this patent marking a milestone in sustainable energy solutions. The company is actively engaging with industry leaders to bring this technology to the market, with potential applications in AI data centers, bitcoin mining, and green fuels production. In summary, Magma Power LLC s patented technology offers a groundbreaking method for hydrogen production using geothermal heat from magma reservoirs. This innovation has the potential to revolutionize the energy landscape by providing a cleaner, more sustainable source of hydrogen for various industries.

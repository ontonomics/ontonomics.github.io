Steem Price Tops $0 . 17 on Top Exchanges ( STEEM )

Steem (STEEM) experienced a 2.6% decline against the U.S. dollar in the past 24 hours, with a weekly drop of 12.9%. One Steem coin is valued at approximately $0.17 or 0.00000276 BTC. Steem s market cap stands at $79.36 million, with $20.37 million traded on exchanges in the last day. Comparatively, Bitcoin (BTC) saw a 0.1% increase, Dogecoin (DOGE) fell by 1.3%, and Bitcoin Cash (BCH) decreased by 0.8%. Other cryptocurrencies like UNUS SED LEO (LEO), Litecoin (LTC), Ethereum Classic (ETC), Monero (XMR), Bitcoin SV (BSV), and NyanCoin (NYAN) also saw declines ranging from 3.1% to 6.2%. Steem is a PoW coin using the PoS hashing algorithm, with a total supply of 472,852,171 coins. It operates on a social media platform where users earn rewards by posting and upvoting content. Steem can be bought using Bitcoin or Ethereum on exchanges and then converted to Steem dollars or Steem power. Steem s block time is 3 seconds, with 90% of the block reward allocated to a vest fund for curators and miners, and the remaining 10% made liquid for content creators.

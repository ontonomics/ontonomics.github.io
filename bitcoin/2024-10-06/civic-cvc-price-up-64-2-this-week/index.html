Civic ( CVC ) Price Up 64 . 2 % This Week

Civic (CVC), a decentralized identity system, experienced a significant 11.2% increase in value against the US dollar in a single day ending October 6th. With a market capitalization of $206.77 million and $637.45 million worth of Civic traded on exchanges in the last day, the cryptocurrency has seen a 64.2% rise over the past week. One Civic token is currently valued at approximately $0.21 or 0.00000331 BTC on cryptocurrency exchanges. In comparison, similar cryptocurrencies have shown mixed performance: Aidi Finance (AIDI) and Zoo Token (ZOOT) both traded 2.2% lower against the dollar, while Hokkaidu Inu (HOKK) and P2P Solutions foundation (P2PS) saw gains of 1.2% and 0.7%, respectively. Lumi Credits (LUMI) and MVP Coin (MVP) also traded up, with LUMI at 8.9% and MVP at 0.6%. Civic was launched on July 12th, 2017, with a total supply of 1 billion tokens. The cryptocurrency s official channels include a Reddit community, Twitter account (@civickey), and Facebook page. To trade Civic, investors must first acquire Bitcoin or Ethereum using a USD-based exchange like Gemini, Coinbase, or GDAX. They can then use their acquired cryptocurrency to purchase Civic on the same exchanges. Civic s official website is www.civic.com, and its decentralized identity system offers secure, blockchain-based identity verification. The Ethereum token (CVC) facilitates identity verification, rewards users, and provides access to Civic platform features.

Multichain was launched on December 15th, 2021, with a total supply of 100 million tokens and a circulating supply of 18.36 million. The official platforms for Multichain include its message board, Twitter, Facebook, and website. Multichain is an open-source protocol designed to enable interoperability among diverse blockchains, supporting Ethereum-like chains, Bitcoin types, and COSMOS chains. It offers services like a Bridge, Router, and Crosschain NFT Bridge and Router for secure and cost-effective cross-chain transfers of assets. The governance of the community and ecosystem is managed through the $MULTI token. To trade Multichain, investors must first purchase Ethereum or Bitcoin using a U.S. dollar-based exchange, and then use those coins to buy Multichain on the same exchange.

Uquid Coin Reaches Market Capitalization of $27 . 30 Million ( UQC )

Uquid Coin (UQC) experienced a 1.2% increase in value against the dollar during the 24-hour period ending on October 6th, with a total market cap of $27.30 million. Approximately $304,223.24 worth of UQC was traded on exchanges in the last day. One UQC token is priced at about $2.73 or 0.00004371 BTC on major cryptocurrency platforms. Over the past week, UQC has seen a 28.6% decrease in value against the dollar. In comparison, Aidi Finance (AIDI) traded down 2.2% against the dollar, Zoo Token (ZOOT) also decreased by 2.2% and CareCoin (CARES) followed suit with a 2.2% drop. Hokkaidu Inu (HOKK) saw a 1.2% increase, while Jeff in Space (JEFF) and Lumi Credits (LUMI) experienced a 2.2% and 8.9% decrease, respectively. AXIA Coin (AXC) and MVP Coin (MVP) had minor changes of 0.1% and 0.6% increases, respectively. P2P Solutions foundation (P2PS) saw a 0.7% increase. Uquid Coin was launched on September 8th, 2017, with a total supply of 40 million tokens and a circulating supply of 10 million tokens. The Uquid Coin team plans to leverage the Ethereum blockchain to enhance their services, introducing features like visa credit cards, a wallet, an exchange, a Social Fund Management Campaign, and a marketplace. To trade UQC, investors must first acquire Bitcoin or Ethereum using a U.S. dollar-based exchange such as Changelly, GDAX, or Gemini.

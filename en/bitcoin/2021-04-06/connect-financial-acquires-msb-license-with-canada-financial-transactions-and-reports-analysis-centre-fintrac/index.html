Connect Financial Acquires MSB License with Canada Financial Transactions and Reports Analysis Centre ( FINTRAC )

Canadian credit card firm Connect Financial has become the first company to launch a Crypto-Asset based Credit Card that is issuable throughout the world. The company has been given an MSB licence with Canada's financial reporting analysis centre ( FINTRAC).

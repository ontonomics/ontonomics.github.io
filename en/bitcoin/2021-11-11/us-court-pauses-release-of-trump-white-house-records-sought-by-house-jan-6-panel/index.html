US Court pauses release of Trump White House records sought by House Jan 6 panel

The US Federal Appeals Court has temporarily paused the handover of records sought by the House Select Committee investigating the Capitol riot, one day before some of them are due to be transferred from the National Archives, reports say. A temporary injunction has been issued.
